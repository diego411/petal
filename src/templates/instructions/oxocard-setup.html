Oxocard Setup

WORK IN PROGRESS



To set up the

Components
  • Plug the Plant Cartridge into the Oxocard Connect interface like this
  • Plug the wires into the Plant Cartridge
    • There are two inputs for the wires on the Plant Cartridge
    • Use the one labeled with "IN"
  • Connect the Oxocard to a power source using a USB-C cable
  • The Oxocard might download some data on startup and try to connect to memorized WIFI networks which might take a few minutes
  • Once the Oxocard is fully started up you should see a screen similar to this one:
  • Navigate to the menu entry called "WIFI" using the buttons under the screen
  • Confirm by clicking the middle button
  • Follow the instructions to connect to the WIFI
  • Once the Oxocard is connected to the WIFI, simply click the menu entry "START"
  • Your Oxocard should now show up here:
  • If your Oxocard does not show up above, try to unplug it from the power source and plug it in again, then click the menu entry "START" once again
  • Should there be any complications with the steps, please contact Diego under this E-Mail address: dlonghi1@smail.uni-koeln.de
