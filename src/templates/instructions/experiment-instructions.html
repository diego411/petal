Emotion Experiment

  • The goal of this experiment is to collect plant signal data while the participants watch a series of emotionally charged videos
  • The face of the participant will be recorded through a webcam and an emotion recognition model will be used to extract the currently felt emotion of the participant
  • At the same time, a sensor connected to a nearby plant will record the plant signals
  • The video feed from the webcam will not be saved by us
  • The only saved data entails the extracted emotions and the plant signals
  • The collected data will then be used to train machine learning models

To conduct this experiment you need the following requirements:

  • an Oxocard
  • a Plant Cartridge and the appropriate wires
  • a basil plant
  • a webcam

Experiment Setup

  • We highly recommend conducting the experiment on a desktop computer and not on your smartphone
  • Connect the Oxocard to the power source and restart it
  • Connect the red wire to one of the plant's leaves
  • Plug the black wire into the earth
  • Place the plant next to you and sit in front of your webcam
  • We ask you to do this experiment in a well-lit room
  • Additionally, please make sure no one else is in the room for the time of the experiment to minimize noise

Conducting the Experiment

  • In the following, you will be redirected to a site displaying a video for you to watch
  • On the right side, you will see your webcam feed with annotations displaying the predicted emotions from the facial recognition model
  • Under the webcam, the collected data from the Oxocard will be displayed in a graph
  • Once you enter the site, please allow the use of the camera in your web browser
  • In the dropdown, please select your Oxocard (if not already selected)
    • Should your Oxocard not show up, please restart it, refresh the web browser, and try again
  • Then once you are ready, simply press the "Start" button
  • The video will then start
  • Simply watch the video until the end and press the "Stop" button once the video finishes
  • That's it! Thank you for participating in the experiment!
